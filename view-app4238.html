SemiRestore is a Cydia Eraser alternative program to semi-restore your device like it was brand new. SemiRestore requires you to have OpenSSH installed for it to work. Note: this is an older project so newer jailbreaks after iOS 9.1 do not have full support, however for iOS 11, Electra comes with its own version of SemiRestore known as Rollectra.
